Over the course of the past ~5 months, while I was completing my Master's degree, I had the oppurutunity to work part-time as pentester for the company Tenax Solutions. During my time with Tenax, I led eight different internal and external penetration tests. Throughout these engagements I consistently challenged myself to learn new attack paths and create new tools. 

I am incredibly greatful for the team at Tenax giving me both the freedom and mentorship to become a better security professional. Having the chance to go hands on with numerous real world environments gave me invaluable experience. I can't wait to contiune my journey in cyber security and expand my understanding of red teaming concepts. Please follow along with me on this page as I continue to share my growth.
